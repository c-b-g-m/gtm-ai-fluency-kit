# Start Here

## Pick Your Path

**No terminal, no Python** (recommended for most GTM professionals):
Go to `QUICKSTART-MANUAL.md`. Copy the templates, score yourself against the rubric, and fill in your plan. **About 30–45 minutes total** (20–30 min assessment + 15 min learning plan).

**Faster with a script** (if you're comfortable with Python):
Go to `BUILD.md`. Run one command to get pre-filled starter files, then complete them manually. **About 35–50 minutes total** (5 min setup + 30–45 min to fill in the generated files).

---

## How the Two Documents Fit Together

You will produce two documents. They build on each other:

**Document 1 — Assessment** (`templates/assessment-template.md`)
Score yourself across 4 dimensions. Identify your two lowest scores. Write a 30-day action starter. This is your diagnosis.

**Document 2 — Learning Plan** (`templates/learning-plan-template.md`)
Take your two gap areas from the assessment and build them into a full 30/60/90 plan with concrete milestones, measurable signals, and an evidence tracker. This is your prescription.

The assessment feeds the learning plan. You fill in Document 1 first, then carry your gaps and 30-day actions into Document 2. They are not the same document — the assessment is a snapshot, the learning plan is your working roadmap.

---

## What to Do When You're Done

Once your learning plan is complete:

1. **Review it against your JD.** Every milestone should map to a real responsibility or success metric you're already evaluated on.
2. **Run the non-generic check** (in `QUICKSTART-MANUAL.md` Step 6). If any answer is no, revise before moving on.
3. **Put it somewhere you'll see it.** Notion, a pinned doc, a recurring calendar reminder — wherever your actual work lives. A plan you can't see isn't a plan.
4. **Schedule a 30-day check-in.** Block 20 minutes on your calendar for 30 days from now. Come back with one artifact that shows movement on your top gap dimension.
5. **Share it with your team** (optional) — use `FACILITATOR-LITE.md` to run the same process as a 30-minute group session.

## The Workflow (Both Paths)

1. **Pick your function track** — Marketing, Sales and RevOps, or Support and Customer Success
2. **Complete a baseline assessment** — Score yourself across 4 dimensions using the rubric and calibrated examples
3. **Identify your gaps** — Your lowest two scores are your 30-day focus
4. **Build your 30/60/90 plan** — Concrete milestones tied to your JD and a real workflow you own
5. **Run the non-generic check** — Make sure every action names a workflow, a measurable signal, and an accountable owner
6. **Schedule your 30-day check-in** — Block time now, before you close the tab

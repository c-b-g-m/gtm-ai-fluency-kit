# Sales and RevOps AI Fluency 30/60/90 Plan

## Role Context

Audience: sales, account executives, and RevOps practitioners.

Primary outcomes:
- Improve deal preparation quality and speed
- Build repeatable account research and follow-up workflows
- Increase confidence in AI-assisted forecasting and prioritization

## 30 Days (Foundation)

- Document 3 recurring workflows (account research, call prep, follow-up drafting).
- Build one structured pre-call AI workflow.
- Define a quality checklist for factual accuracy and message relevance.
- Track one correction example each week where AI output was adjusted.

Evidence:
- Pre-call workflow template
- Before/after call prep output sample
- Weekly correction log

## 60 Days (Operationalization)

- Standardize one multi-step workflow connecting data inputs to outreach outputs.
- Add validation checks for pipeline claims and account context.
- Measure time saved and quality impact on prep and follow-up.
- Publish one shared team template.

Evidence:
- Shared workflow playbook
- Validation checklist
- Impact summary (time and quality)

## 90 Days (Scale)

- Automate one low-risk recurring motion with clear human review points.
- Train peers on the workflow and document guardrails.
- Define accountability standards for AI-assisted customer-facing materials.

Evidence:
- Workflow map v2 with review gates
- Team enablement session notes
- Sales diligence statement

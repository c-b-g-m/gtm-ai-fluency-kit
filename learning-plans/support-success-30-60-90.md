# Support and Success AI Fluency 30/60/90 Plan

## Role Context

Audience: customer support and customer success practitioners.

Primary outcomes:
- Improve response quality and consistency
- Build repeatable triage and resolution-support workflows
- Strengthen diligence for customer trust and escalation handling

## 30 Days (Foundation)

- Document 3 recurring workflows (ticket triage, response drafting, account summary prep).
- Build one reusable context packet template for complex tickets.
- Create a response-quality checklist (accuracy, tone, policy alignment).
- Track one example per week where AI output required correction.

Evidence:
- Context packet template
- Before/after response examples
- Weekly correction log

## 60 Days (Operationalization)

- Standardize one AI-assisted triage and response workflow.
- Add escalation guardrails and handoff triggers.
- Measure first-response quality and cycle-time changes.
- Publish one internal team template.

Evidence:
- Triage workflow playbook
- Escalation guardrail checklist
- Metrics snapshot

## 90 Days (Scale)

- Deploy one low-risk always-on support aid with human oversight.
- Train peers on workflow usage and edge-case handling.
- Define accountability and disclosure norms for AI-assisted support interactions.

Evidence:
- Workflow map v2 with escalation points
- Team enablement notes
- Support diligence statement
